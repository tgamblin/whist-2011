Guidelines

  • All submissions should be in PDF format and follow the ACM SIG proceedings template.
  • Length is limited to 8 pages, but up to four extra pages may be added for a fee.
    • We charge $250 each for pages 9 and 10, and $500 each for pages 11 and 12.
  • Submissions should represent original, substantive research results.
  • At least one author of every accepted paper is required to attend WHIST 2011 in order for the paper to be included in the final proceedings.
  • Workshop proceedings will be made available on the web in electronic format.
